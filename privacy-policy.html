Privacy Policy

Orto & Trauma Content Publisher

Effective Date: April 7, 2026 · Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Orto & Trauma Content Publisher ("the App"), operated by GenAI Sapiens ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application to publish content to TikTok and other social media platforms.

The App is an internal automation tool that publishes educational medical content — specifically Instagram carousels, TikTok photo posts, and podcast episodes — on behalf of the Orto & Trauma with Dr. Polotrauma brand.

Contact: gerard@genaisapiens.com

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and processes the following categories of information:

2.1 TikTok Account Information

  • TikTok user ID and display name (obtained via OAuth 2.0 authorization)
  • Access tokens required to publish content on your behalf

2.2 Content Data

  • Image files (carousel slides) generated for publication
  • Post descriptions and captions
  • Audio files associated with posts

2.3 Technical Data

  • API response data (post IDs, publication status, error logs)
  • Timestamps of publishing actions

We do not collect personal data from TikTok viewers or followers. We do not collect analytics, comments, or engagement data from TikTok.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Publish photo posts and associated content to your TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API
  • Monitor publishing status and troubleshoot failed uploads
  • Maintain internal logs of published content for editorial tracking

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to content publication.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties.

Your data may be shared only with the following service providers, strictly for the purpose of content publication:

Service Purpose Data Shared
TikTok (via Content Posting API) Publish photo posts Images, captions, audio
Cloudinary Image hosting CDN Slide images
Google Drive File storage Content files
ElevenLabs Text-to-speech generation Audio scripts

All third-party services are bound by their own privacy policies and terms of service.

5. Data Retention

  • Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed as required by the TikTok API. Tokens are revoked upon request or when the App is deauthorized.
  • Published content metadata (post IDs, timestamps) is retained in internal records for editorial reference.
  • Content files (images, audio) are stored in Google Drive and may be deleted at our discretion after publication.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure storage of API credentials and access tokens
  • HTTPS encryption for all API communications
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke access to your TikTok account by deauthorizing the App in your TikTok settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data by contacting us at gerard@genaisapiens.com
  • Request information about what data we hold related to your account

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
